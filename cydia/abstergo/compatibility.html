Abstergo is tested to be compatible with the following tweaks:

  • IntelliScreenX: Individual clearing in the Notification Center only works when it's configured as the only visible page.
  • Emblem: Tap and hold on the notification icon to show the reminder options.
  • LockInfo 5
  • Reveal
  • biteSMS
  • Messages+
  • Atom
  • Bulletin
  • BTNotificationEnabler
  • QuickReply for WhatsApp and Viber
  • Velox (coming soon)

This list is not complete. There are many other tweaks that are compatible with Abstergo.

If you find a compatibility issue with another tweak, contact us from Abstergo's settings and we'll do our best to fix it.
